# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent while giving direct technical feedback
- Focus on the work and the impact on users
- Welcome questions from contributors at different experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption of community discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and pull requests that violate it.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact `support@a11y.itisuniqueofficial.com`.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project.
